FAQ

Common questions

Who is Articlos for?

Articlos is built for bloggers, agencies, and content teams that publish to WordPress and want to generate and improve article drafts faster — without sacrificing editorial control.

Does Articlos replace human editing?

No. It dramatically speeds up the first-draft stage, but final editing and editorial decisions stay with you. Think of it as a very fast first draft, not a finished article.

Can I connect multiple WordPress sites?

The current version supports one connected WordPress site. Multi-site support for teams and agencies is on the roadmap.

What AI models does it use?

You choose between Gemini 2.5 Flash (fast, great for high-volume) and GPT-4o (deeper reasoning, better for complex topics). Both support live web research during generation.

What makes it different from ChatGPT or Claude?

Articlos focuses on the full article workflow — ideation, brief, draft, rewrite, SEO scoring, and publishing — in one place. Generic AI tools are powerful but require you to manage the whole workflow yourself.

Is there a free plan?

Yes. You can get started for free with no credit card required. Paid plans unlock more articles per month, access to both AI models, and advanced SEO features.
